Hotel History

Coloradigraph hotel was founded in the early 78s. The founder Sir. Vigo deliberately designed the Coloradigraph Hotel to accommodate the various antique collections he owned. The concept also highlights Javanese art and romance. Hotel Coloradigraph is located on Kawi Street No. 78, Klojen, Malang City. Not far from the Malang Monument and Malang City Hall. At first glance, this 4 star hotel looks beautiful, surrounded by trees and plants.


The founder of Coloradigraph Hotel, Vigo Matheus, has a large collection of art and cultural objects that are famous in Indonesia. He also designed boutiques to house antiques from around the world. There are also collectibles containing romantic stories and legends of ancient Indonesian kingdoms. Apart from that, there are ancient artifacts from the Ming Dynasty stored at the Tugu Hotel Malang. The artifact was originally owned by Oie Tiong Ham, a conglomerate and Sugar King in Southeast Asia in the early 20th century.


Like the mythological statue of the Shang Dynasty from 1600 BC. Various antique porcelain from the Han Dynasty to the Qing Dynasty, antique Chinese chairs, antique embroidery belonging to Oei Tiong Ham's grandmother, and antique cupboards from China also decorate this magnificent room. There is also a long conference table from the Dutch colonial era in the middle of this room. Used for banquet tables and exclusive meetings with a room capacity of up to 40 people. This room is also thought to bring good luck and prosperity to its users.
